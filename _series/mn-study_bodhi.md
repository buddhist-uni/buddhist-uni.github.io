A series of nearly three hundred lectures on the entire *Majjhima Nikāya* delivered by Bhikkhu Bodhi over a thirteen-year period at Bodhi Monastery.

The original, nine-part series covered a selection of suttas organized thematically, and so did not go in order. Parts 10 and 11 of this series do cover the remaining suttas, so if there are any suttas in the MN you'd like Bhikkhu Bodhi to explain, you'll find them somewhere below!

You can find the series as mp3 files [on Google Drive](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1JlG4P34WCAIMugD3WFcOrndq2pc1P7zO){:target="_blank" ga-event-value="1"}.
The first half live [at the Bodhi Monastery website](https://bodhimonastery.org/a-systematic-study-of-the-majjhima-nikaya.html){:target="_blank" ga-event-value="1"}, which includes [the introductory lecture](http://bodhimonastery.org/courses/MN/MP3/M0000_Intro.mp3){:target="_blank" ga-event-value="0.2"}, not listed below.
The latter half of the series [can be seen on YouTube](https://youtube.com/playlist?list=PL23DE0292227250FA){:target="_blank" ga-event-value="0.6"}.

Many of Bhikkhu Bodhi's earlier lectures on the Majjhima Nikaya can be found [on DharmaSeed](https://dharmaseed.org/teacher/33/?page=2&sort=rec_date&page_items=10&search=MN){:target="_blank" ga-event-value="1"}.
