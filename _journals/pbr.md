Pali Buddhist Review was produced from 1976 to 1982 (to Vol. 6, no.2) by the Pali Buddhist Union, and was edited by Russell Webb. In 1983, it was superceded by [Buddhist Studies Review]({% link _journals/bsr.md %}).
