 This resource is a visual guide to Japanese Buddhist and Shintō deities, featuring over 70 annotated images from the 12th to 20th centuries. It serves as a comprehensive resource for students, educators, and enthusiasts interested in the rich tapestry of Japanese religious iconography and its cultural contexts.
