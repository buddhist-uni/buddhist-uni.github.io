An open-source platform aggregating Buddhist texts from 440+ digital sources across 30 languages. Features full-text multilingual search, parallel reading, a knowledge graph with 9,600+ entities, RAG-based AI Q&A grounded in canonical texts, 6 integrated dictionaries, and an IIIF manuscript viewer.
