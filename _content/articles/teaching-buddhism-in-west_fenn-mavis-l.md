> As a primary focus, “Historical” appeared in every answer, but in only
three cases was it listed as the sole focus (10%).
It was found in combination
most often with “Textual” (61%) and “Anthropological” (42%). “Contemporary” appeared only eleven times (36%) and "linguistic" seven (23%). 

The results of a small, online survey of Anglophone Buddhist Studies departments.
