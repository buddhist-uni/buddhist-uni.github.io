> With a focus on the Tripiṭaka translation, this article examines facets of academic life and intellectual work of A.P.
> de Zoysa (1890–1968), who single-handedly embarked on translating the Tripiṭaka into Sinhala and both the religious and historical significance of that project in enhancing Buddhist understanding of the Buddha’s teachings by reaching out to a broader local, vernacular audience.
