> We conduct a spatially-explicit analysis of Sanjiangyuan for two periods, 1990–2000 and 2010–2015, to identify and quantify the influence of the presence and spatial distributions of Buddhist monasteries on land use compared to village communities that lack monasteries.
> We found that the presence of monasteries is highly correlated with the preservation of natural ecosystems, specifically of grasslands and forests.
