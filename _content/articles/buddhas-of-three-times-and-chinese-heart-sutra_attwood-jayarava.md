> The phrase *tryadhvavyavastithāḥ sarvabuddhāḥ*  is unambiguously a Chinese idiom translated into Sanskrit in ignorance of Sanskrit Prajnāpāramitā conventions.
> This proves that the Heart Sutra was composed in Chinese.

Well, it proves that _this_ Sanskrit version was a translation from the Chinese.
Establishing that the Chinese Heart Sūtra didn't have a _different_ Sanskrit original is a different matter.
