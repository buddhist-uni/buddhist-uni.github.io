> Efforts to import the Theravāda
monastic system in a partial manner did not succeed. The system eventually
had to be swallowed whole, though rendered more palatable by specific
innovations.

The inaugural article for the Journal of Global Buddhism covers the transplanting of the Thai Forest Tradition to Britain and covers in detail how this new sect uses the rhetoric of its ancient roots to appeal to modern sensibilities.
