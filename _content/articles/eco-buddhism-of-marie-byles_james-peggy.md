> Marie Beuzeville Byles (1900–1979) was a key figure in the historical development of Buddhism in Australia, and the nation's conservation movement.
> From the 1940s she beganto develop an eco-Buddhist worldview and Buddhist environmental ethic that she applied in her day-to-day conservation activities and articulated over the course of four books on Buddhism and dozens of published articles.
