> In response, Buddhist leaders divided traditional Buddhist cosmology and practices into the newly constructed categories 'superstition' and 'religion.'
Superstition was deemed 'not really Buddhism' and purged, while the remainder of Buddhism was made to accord with Westernized ideas of 'religion.'
