This volume contains three classical works that cover the history of Pali literature in Sri Lanka, Burma, and Southeast Asia. All three works, previously published independently, were compiled into a single bound volume in 2004. Each work covers distinct literary histories and styles. They are:
1. The Pali Literature of Ceylon by G. P. Malalasekera
2. The Pali Literature of Burma by Mabel Haynes Bode
3. The Pali Literature of South-East Asia by H. Saddhātissa
