This volume contains three classical works: 
1. The Pali Literature of Ceylon by G. P. Malalasekera
2. The Pali Literature of Burma by Mabel Haynes Bode
3. The Pali Literature of South-East Asia by H. Saddhātissa

All three works, previously published independently, are here compiled into a single volume.
