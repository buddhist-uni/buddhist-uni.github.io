> Call it hyper-vision. Call it unrealistic. I follow the gypsy air.

> All our two times two and definitions of photosynthesis, our political understanding and complaining cannot free us from the boundary of a dusty earth and so much brown of it.

> Forget what the experts say about Autism; their knowledge is anything but solid. Autism flows: it doesn’t settle; it doesn’t shape.
