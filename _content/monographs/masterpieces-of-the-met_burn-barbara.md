> While this book does not present a complete and balanced history of art, nearly every culture and period are well represented by objects of the highest quality [...] with the works arranged chronologically, so that the reader may be led logically through the history of human artistic accomplishment. Appearing alongside paintings and sculpture are works of decorative art, such as ceramics, tapestries, arms and armor, musical instruments, costumes, and great pieces of furniture.
