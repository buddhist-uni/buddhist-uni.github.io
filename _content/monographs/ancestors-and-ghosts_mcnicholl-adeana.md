> These tales provide us with a glimpse of the ways that cosmologies do not materialize fully formed but are cumulatively built over time, subject to continual reshaping in different contexts.
In this book I argue that preta narratives do not merely illustrate a changing cosmological system after the fact but played a crucial role in the process of the formation of that system
