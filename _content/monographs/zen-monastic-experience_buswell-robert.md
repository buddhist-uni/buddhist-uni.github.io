> It is three in the morning and another day has begun at the Korean Buddhist monastery of Songwang-sa...

Based on years of engagement with Korean Buddhist history as well as observation of this particular monastery, this monograph describes, in intimate and honest detail, what it is like to be a monk in Korea.
