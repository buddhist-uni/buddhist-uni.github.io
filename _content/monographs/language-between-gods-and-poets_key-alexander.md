> Whenever I say “mental content” in English, the Arabic word is *maʿnā*, and whenever I say “accurate,” “accuracy,” or “accurately” in English, the Arabic word is *ḥaqīqah*.


An exploration of medieval, Arabic philosophy of language and in particular their attempts to explain the "miracle" of poetry.
