> Gazing at a massive dam as it holds forth against the huge forces of a river, can we doubt that we are witnessing a marvelous feat of engineering, a triumph of human ingenuity over nature? Yet what a receipt does is no less remarkable and no less powerful, even if it is less immediately apparent, for it is holding forth against the ravages of time.

A history of "the document" and writing in all its forms.
