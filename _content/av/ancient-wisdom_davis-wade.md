> Kinesthetic genius exists. Intuitive genius exists. It's simply that we may not always identify it as 'genius.'

Josh interviews famed anthropologist Wade Davis about the 'genius' of other cultures and how anthropology can teach us to listen more deeply to each other and to thus expand our notion of what it's possible for a human to be.
