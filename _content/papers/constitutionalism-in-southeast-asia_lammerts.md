> The original text of the law is inscribed in “letters as big as a cow” on the boundary-wall of the universe, from which it is transcribed and transmitted to the human realm by [Manu], who magically retrieved the alien text during the reign of the first king Mahāsammata.
In addition to its origins in outer space, accessible only to superhuman cosmonauts, *dhamma-sattha* texts are engaged in a complex relationship with Buddhist vinaya and sutra texts, which are frequently deployed, although sometimes with substantial changes...

A brief look at the actual legal history of Burma shows that the supposed constitutionality of *dhammasattha* treatises was always in negotiation with the proclamations of kings, showing how power and rhetorical authority were actually constituted in premodern, mainland Southeast Asia.
