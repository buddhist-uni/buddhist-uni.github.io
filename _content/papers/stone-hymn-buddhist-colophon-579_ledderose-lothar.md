This book chapter details the archeological history of Stone Hymn, engraved Buddhist scriptures on Mount Tie. The chapter mentions early Chinese archiologists Huang Yi (1744–1802) and Ruan Yuan (1764–1849), as well as outlining the work done in recent years by the research group of the Heidelberger Akademie der Wissenschaften.
