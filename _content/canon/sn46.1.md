> ... based upon virtue, established upon virtue, a bhikkhu develops and cultivates the seven factors of enlightenment...

Dragons nurture their strength in the Himalayas, then enter the rivers and reach the ocean. So too, a mendicant nurtures ethics and then develops the seven awakening factors to reach nibbÄna.
