> Form, feeling, perception, choices, and consciousness. These are called the things that should be completely understood.
And what is complete understanding? The ending of greed, hate, and delusion.

Even the Stream Enterer and Anāgāmīn understanding of the Aggregates is provisional.
Only the Arahant completely understands the Five Aggregates.
