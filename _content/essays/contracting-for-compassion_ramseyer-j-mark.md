> Without a coercive village structure to enforce giving, the low-tension temples found themselves without their effective retainer.
> With the first-best contract unavailable, many temples have turned to fee-for-service arrangements of which the abortion-related ritual is merely the most notorious.
> Ironically, the new environment presents an entirely different challenge: temples now find themselves competing with internet-based priest-dispatch services.
