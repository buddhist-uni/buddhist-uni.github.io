>  Seemingly, we might have boundless knowledge, all derived from study and
reflection, but if our fundamental character is not attuned to the Dharma, we will not
tame the enemy, the destructive emotions.

Longchen Rabjam urges practitioners to go beyond mere study and rituals, to rest in pure awareness free of grasping, dedicate all virtue to others, and thereby realize the ultimate meaning of the Dharma.
