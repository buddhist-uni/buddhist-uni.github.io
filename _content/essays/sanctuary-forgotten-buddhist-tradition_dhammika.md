> An accused who was able to flee to
the nearest monastery would be protected from such mob justice. Sanctuary would give the person an 
opportunity to explain himself and allow his accusers to calm down so the facts could be examined more
objectively. 

> The Pali terms equivalent to sanctuary would be abhayatthana or pujjatthana. Sanctuary in Buddhist
monasteries had a long history in Sri Lanka lasting for at least 1,000 years.
