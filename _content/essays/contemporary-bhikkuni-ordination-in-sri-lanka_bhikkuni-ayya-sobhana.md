> The bhikkunis are
venerated and supported by laity, but still materially
quite poor compared to their male counterparts.

This blog post discusses the history and challenges of bhikkhuni ordination in Sri Lanka, focusing on the contemporary efforts to revive full ordination for women in the Theravada tradition. The essay also addresses the resistance and support for this practice within the country.
