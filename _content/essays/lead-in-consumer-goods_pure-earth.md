> 18% of samples exceeded relevant health guidelines or regulatory limits. Metal foodware (51%), ceramics (45%), paint (41%), toys (13%), and cosmetics (12%) were the most common culprits.

While the developed world has (mostly!) eliminated lead from consumer goods, lower-income countries are still producing contaminated items.
