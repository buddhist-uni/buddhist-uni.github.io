Here the great Tibetan Buddhist master Patrul Rinpoche outlines the general confession given by the five great sages, 
"the god Yaśasvī Varapāla (*grags ldan mchog skyong*), the nāga king Takṣaka (*klu rgyal ’jog po*), the yakṣa Ulkāmukha (*skar mda’ gdong*), the rākṣasa Matyaupāyika (*blo gros thabs ldan*), and the human being Licchavi Vimalakīrti (*dri med grags pa*)."
