The Metropolitan Museum of Art is an encyclopedic art museum in New York City. It is the fourth-largest and fifth-most-visited museum in the world.

The Met publishes an annual journal and a quarterly bulletin as well as a variety of art and research books showcasing their various collections, exhibits, and programs.
