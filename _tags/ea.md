Taishō 125 (增壹阿含經 *Zēngyī Āhán Jīng*), roughly parallel to the Aṅguttara Nikāya of Southeast Asia, contains [early](/tags/ebts) (as well as some late) sūtras of the Dharmaguptakas preserved in Chinese translation.
