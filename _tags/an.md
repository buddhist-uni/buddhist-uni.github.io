The numerical basket is the last of the four main *nikāya*s of the *Sutta Piṭaka*, containing practical and memorable teachings from the early sangha. The collection is roughly parallel to the {% include tag-link.html text="Ekottara Āgama" tag="ea" %} preserved in China.
