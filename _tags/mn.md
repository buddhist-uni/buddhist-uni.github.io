The *Majjhima Nikāya* is the second part of the *Sutta Piṭaka*, containing the discourses and stories of the early sangha of a medium length. It is roughly parallel to the {% include tag-link.html text="Madhyama Āgama" tag="ma" %} collection preserved in China.
