MN 20

Vitakkasaṇṭhāna Sutta (The Relaxation of Thoughts)

⭐ Recommended
This is a piece of exceptional quality, recommended for everyone.
Online pdf

In a practical meditation teaching, the Buddha describes five progressive approaches to arresting unwanted thoughts.

The Majjhima Nikāya Buddhist Meditation Buddhist Ethics

Translated by Ajahn Geoff Ṭhānissaro. Published in 1997.
