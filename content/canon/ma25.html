MA 25

Discourse on the Five Ways of Putting an End to Anger

🥇 Best Of The Library
One of the best works in the library, "MA 25" was featured in the May 2020 newsletter and is highly recommended for all.
Online pdf

Venerable Shariputra explains five ways to quell anger through wise attention, giving five memorable similes on being determined to find the good in everyone.

Buddhist Ethics The Madhyama Āgama Imagery in the EBTs

Featured in the course, " Buddhist Ethics."

Translated by Thích Nhất Hạnh. Published in 2006.
