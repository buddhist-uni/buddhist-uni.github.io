SA 267

The Second Discourse on Not Knowing

Online

Have you seen the variegated and different colours of a caraṇa bird?

The Buddha encourages the monks to investigate the five aggregates, giving a few colorful similes to illustrate their nature.

Imagery in the EBTs The Saṃyukta Āgama Buddhist Philosophy

Featured in the course, " Imagery in the Early Buddhist Texts."

Translated by Bhikkhu Anālayo. Published in 2013.
