Two Sūtras in the Chinese Saṃyuktāgama without Direct Pāli Parallels

Some Remarks on how to identify Later Additions to the Corpus

By Marcus Bingenheimer

Online pdf

Not all Āgamas are early. In this paper, Bingenheimer shows us how two sutras without parallels in the Pāli can be shown as likely to be later additions to the canon.

The Āgamas The Saṃyukta Āgama

Published in 2013 in Buddhist Studies Review.
