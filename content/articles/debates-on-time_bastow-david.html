Debates on Time in the Kathāvatthu

By David Bastow

⭐ Recommended
This is a piece of exceptional quality, recommended for everyone.
pdf pdf

A guided reading of a small section of the Abhidhamma related to how different Indian schools explained time and a hypothesis about how they may have debated the topic amongst themselves.

Indian Buddhism Early Sects

Published in 1996 in Buddhist Studies Review.
