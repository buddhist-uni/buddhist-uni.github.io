Did the Buddha Exist

By Alexander Wynne

📦 In The Archives
This work is only recommended for those with a special interest.
Online pdf

Argues that the Early Buddhist Texts could not have been “designed by committee” and on this basis argues that there must have been a historical Buddha.

The Early Buddhist Texts The Buddha

Published in 2019 in The Journal of the Oxford Center for Buddhist Studies.
