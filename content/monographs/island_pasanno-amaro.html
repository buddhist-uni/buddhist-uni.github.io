The Island

An Anthology of the Buddha’s Teachings on Nibbāna

By Ajahn Pasanno and Ajahn Amaro

🥇 Best Of The Library
One of the best works in the library, "The Island" was featured in the May 2020 newsletter and is highly recommended for all.
Online pdf epub

A compendium of ‘essence’ teachings on nibbāna, as they appear in the Pāli Canon and in contemporary traditions. My most highly recommended book for anyone who wants to know what Nibbāna is.

Nibbāna The Function of Buddhism Buddhist Philosophy

Featured in the course, " Nibbāna: The Goal of Buddhist Practice."

Published in 2009 by Amaravati, Hertfordshire, UK.
