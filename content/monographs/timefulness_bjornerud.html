Timefulness

How Thinking Like a Geologist Can Help Save the World

By Marcia Bjornerud

⭐ Recommended
This is a piece of exceptional quality, recommended for everyone.

I yearned to convey to [Charles Darwin] how marvelously his simple idea has flowered and itself evolved, informing countless new fields of inquiry, and to share with him the scientific news that would have eased his troubled mind: Earth is old.

An extraordinary retelling of the history of both the Earth and our understanding of it, which will stretch and stagger your temporal imagination.

The (Human) World
