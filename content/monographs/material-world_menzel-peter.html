Material World

A Global Family Portrait

By Peter Menzel

⭐ Recommended
This is a piece of exceptional quality, recommended for everyone.
Preview WorldCat™
This fine, Copyrighted work cannot be shared in its entirety, however WorldCat™ can help you find a copy at a nearby library or wherever you get books.

Traveling around the globe and living for a week with average families from a variety of countries, sixteen photographers collaborated with thirty families to make this revealing series of portraits.

An acclaimed meditation on our material existence, some of the photos from this book can be previewed on Brain Pickings, Menzel’s Website or on Google Drive.

The (Human) World

Published in 1994 by Counterpoint.
