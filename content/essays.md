Essays are short pieces of writing that were self-published independently. For essays published in periodicals, see [articles]({% link content/articles.md %}). For essays published in edited collections by various authors, see [papers]({% link content/papers.md %}). For essays published in books by the same author, see [excerpts]({% link content/excerpts.md %}). For essays published as booklets, see [booklets]({% link content/booklets.md %}).
